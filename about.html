Our Story

Jaiti was founded in 2023 in Jaipur, India, with a simple yet powerful belief: every child, regardless of their economic background, deserves access to quality education.

What started as a small initiative to help a few children in our community has grown into a movement that has touched the lives of over 50 children and their families. We witnessed firsthand how financial constraints forced bright, eager children to abandon their dreams of education. This reality drove us to action.

Today, Jaiti stands as a beacon of hope for underprivileged children in Jaipur. We don't just teach â€“ we invest in futures. By covering school fees, providing learning materials, and offering dedicated tutoring, we ensure that poverty is never a barrier to knowledge.

Our Mission

To provide free, quality education to underprivileged children in Jaipur and fund their complete schooling, empowering them to build better futures for themselves and their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every child has equal access to education, where financial circumstances don't determine a child's potential, and where learning is a right, not a privilege.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to contribute to our mission. Every form of support makes a real difference in a child's life.

Volunteer Your Time

Share your knowledge by tutoring children in subjects you're passionate about. Even a few hours a week can transform a child's educational journey.

Donate Supplies

Books, stationery, uniforms, and learning materials are always needed. Your contributions directly support our students.

Financial Support

Help us cover school fees, examination costs, and educational materials for children who cannot afford them.

Spread the Word

Share our mission with your network. The more people know about Jaiti, the more children we can reach.
