Education, Not Poverty.

Jaiti Foundation provides completely free education and school support to underprivileged slum children in Jaipur.

Support a Child

Why Jaiti Exists

In many underserved communities, children leave school not because they lack ability, but because poverty limits their opportunities. Jaiti ensures that financial hardship never decides a child’s future.

Our Mission

To provide consistent academic support, school funding, and mentorship so that every child can stay in school and build a better future.

What We Do

Free Tutoring

Daily academic support to help children succeed in school.

School Funding

We cover school fees, books, uniforms, and essential supplies.

Mentorship

Guidance and emotional support to help children stay motivated.
